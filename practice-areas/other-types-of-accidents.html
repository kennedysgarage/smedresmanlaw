Other Types of Accidents

New York is a place where accidents and other unforeseen incidents can and often do happen. In addition to the types of accidents more fully described in the practice areas section of this site, below is a list of other types of cases handled by Steven Smedresman:

  • Auto, taxi, bus, or subway accidents
  • Construction worker's accidents (Labor Law cases)
  • Accidents in buildings, apartments, streets
  • Slip and fall cases caused by icy or defective streets or sidewalks
  • False arrest, false imprisonment
  • Medical malpractice cases, including birth defects, brain damage or Cerebral Palsy
  • Injuries resulting in death or dismemberment
  • Dangerous machines that cause injury
  • Ceiling cave-in cases
  • Elevator and escalator malfunction cases
  • Broken stairs or other dangerous building conditions
  • School accidents
  • Improper or inadequate security leading to criminal attack
