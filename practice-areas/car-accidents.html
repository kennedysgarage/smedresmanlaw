Every New Yorker knows that auto accidents are a sad fact of life in this busy city. But until you or a loved one has actually been involved in an accident it is impossible to realize how physically and financially damaging such events truly are. For three decades the Law Firm of Steven Smedresman has represented injured New Yorkers with their crucially important auto accident cases.

We will be there every step of the way to help. We will initiate a prompt investigation of your case to obtain witness statements and other evidence vital to the case; we will make sure your medical claims are filed with the correct insurance company and that your bills are paid; we will make sure that all possible sources of insurance coverage are looked into and that claims are all put in on a timely basis; we will help you find the right doctor for your case if you don’t have one already, and we will collect all the medical reports to put forth the strongest possible case.

Even if your case involves a hit and run accident or an uninsured car, we can still get you money for your claim. If you’ve been involved in a hit and run accident it is crucial that you contact me immediately, as filing deadlines must be strictly adhered to!

For thirty years the law firm of Steven Smedresman has dedicated itself to fighting for the interests of the injured and hurt, in court and out, to make sure that the insurance company does the one thing it hates the most: Pay full value on the claim.

If you have been injured or if a family member has been injured or killed in an auto accident in New York City, the Bronx, Brooklyn, Queens or in Nassau, Suffolk or Rockland Counties you may well be entitled to collect money damages.
