About Us

For thirty years the law firm of Steven Smedresman, P.C. has battled insurance companies for injured New Yorkers, representing thousands of satisfied clients and collecting millions in settlements along the way. By aggressively representing my client’s interests and by being there 24/7 for each client, the law firm of Steven Smedresman is the firm to hire for your day in court. I know New York, I know New Yorkers, and I know what it takes to win in the Courts of New York.

 

If you have been injured as a result of being hit by a car as a pedestrian; if you have been injured in a car accident; if you have been involved in an accident with a taxi; if you are a construction worker who has been injured by a height-related danger; if you have been hurt in a trip and fall or a slip and fall accident; if you have been a victim of medical malpractice; if you have been hurt by someone else's carelessness and recklessness; contact me for a FREE consultation.

Our Philosophy

My guiding principal has always been that I am here to dedicate 100% of my efforts towards your case. By treating each case with the professional attention it deserves I have proudly represented thousands of clients in Manhattan, the Bronx, Brooklyn, Queens, Staten Island and the surrounding counties, who were thankful they chose the law firm of Steven Smedresman. Your case will be handled by me personally, and not delegated to anyone.

Steven Smedresman

Born and raised in Queens, Steven Smedresman is a proud product of the NYC Board of Education. Mr. Smedresman graduated college from New York University, with Honors, majoring in politics with a minor in economics. Mr. Smedresman received his law degree in 1979 from the Benjamin N. Cardozo Law School, where he was a member of the first graduating class. His entire education and professional life has been in the City of New York.
